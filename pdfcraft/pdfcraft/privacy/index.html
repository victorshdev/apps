Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

At PDFcraft, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the app, such as the photos you select for PDF conversion and the PDF settings you configure. This data is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect certain information through Mixpanel analytics:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent in the app

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app
  • Understand and analyze how you use our app
  • Develop new features, products, and services
  • Analyze app usage patterns through Mixpanel to improve user experience

Analytics (Mixpanel)

We use Mixpanel, a third-party analytics service, to understand how you use our app and to improve user experience. Mixpanel helps us analyze:

  • Which features are used most frequently
  • How users navigate through the app
  • General usage patterns and trends

Information Collected by Mixpanel

  • Usage Data: Actions you take in the app (adding photos, converting to PDF, using the editor)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Anonymous Identifiers: Unique identifiers that do not identify you personally

Important: Mixpanel does not collect your personal information or the content of your photos. All analytics data is anonymized and cannot identify you.

Opt-Out of Analytics

You can opt out of analytics collection at any time by going to Settings → Data Preferences → Usage Analytics and turning it off. This will disable all analytics data collection while still allowing you to use all app features.

For more information about how Mixpanel processes data, please review Mixpanel's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your photos are used solely for PDF conversion and are never uploaded to any server. All processing happens entirely on your device. Generated PDFs are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With Mixpanel for anonymous analytics as described above
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from users of any age. All photo data is processed locally on the device and never stored or transmitted to external servers.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your personal data
  • The right to opt out of analytics

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
